ADMISSION POLICY

Admission to ND-RVMCC Basic Education and College Department is granted with a definite understanding that the student and his/her parents or guardians agree in writting to comply with the scholastic standards of the school and its rules and regulations.

CREDENTIALS FOR ADMISSION AND RE-ADMISSION
(College Department)

INCOMING FRESHMEN:
a. Entrance Test Result
b. Form 138
c. Birth Certificate (SECPA from NSO)
d. Baptismal Certificate (for Catholic)
e. Certificate of Good Moral Character
f. 3 pcs. 2x2 I.D. Picture
g. 1 pc Long Brown Craft Envelope
h. Mailing Envelope and Stamp (Official RVM short envelope)
i. Marriage Certificate (For Marraige)

TRANSFEREES:
Tranferee students are those who wish to enroll after having been enrolled in college at another institution. Former college students returning to ND-RVMCC after having enrolled in other institution are also considered transferees. They are required to submit the following credentials.

a. Entrance Test Result
b. Form 138
c. Birth Certificate (SECPA from NSO)
d. Baptismal Certificate (for Catholic)
e. Certificate of Good Moral Character
f. 3 pcs. 2x2 I.D. Picture
g. 1 pc Long Brown Craft Envelope
h. Mailing Envelope and Stamp (Official RVM short envelope)
i. Marriage Certificate (For Marraige)

SCHOLARSHIP GRANTS

The Notre Dame-RVM College of Cotabato, College Department offers the following scholarships grants:

a. Full Tuition Scholarship is awarded to:
-Valedictorian from school of recognized standing with a minimum of 100 students in the graduating class.
b. Partial Tuition Scholarship is awarded to:
-Salutatorian from school of recognized standing with a minimum of 100 students in the graduating class.
c. Mother Ignacia del Espiritu Santo Scholarship
d. Notre Dame Educational Association (NDEA) Scholarship.
e. ASEE Foundation Scholarship
f. Goverment Recognized Scholarship
